What LinkUp does

Everything your
group needs

One app for events, chats, RSVPs, and more. No group chat chaos. No missed plans.

📅

Group Events

Create events with dates, locations, and details. RSVP with Going, Maybe, or Not Going. Set capacity limits and cutoff deadlines.

💬

Group Chat

Real-time messaging with reactions, replies, polls, and event tags — all in one thread for each group.

🗓️

Unified Calendar

See your personal calendar events alongside group events in one view so you never double-book.

🔗

Easy Group Joining

Join groups instantly by scanning a circular QR code or entering a 6-character code. Private groups require admin approval.

🔔

Smart Notifications

Get notified about new events, RSVPs, reminders, and group activity. Customize or mute per group.

🛡️

Privacy First

Event locations are encrypted end-to-end. Your tokens live in the iOS Keychain. Your data is never sold.
